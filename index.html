Contemplations

Last updated: December 2025

I intend to do what is good, what is true, what works, and what is beautiful. The phrase "opus amoris" is just Latin for "a work of love" (Google Translate would tell you so). What counts as that is as follows: 1) projects made to honor any of the faculties the Lord, Love, has given me, and 2) outputs of the enamored mind
